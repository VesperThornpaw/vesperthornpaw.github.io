The Lore

The Name

    Yes, "Vesper Thornpaw" is the name, so please, if you will, wear it out.

The History

    I've been a furry since I was five or six. My first fursona was an unnamed cheetah when I was in kindergarden. We were dressing like animals for a play and I picked a cheetah. Come to think of it, the rest of my class was technically furry for a day. 'evilgrin.png'
    When I was nine, my interest in the furry fandom itself started with the release of Star Fox 64 on the N64. The idea that animals could walk, talk and act as humans was facinating to me.

The Design

    Midnight-Black and Soft Tawny-Brown coat with lighter underfur. Purple eyes. Purple hair-tips. I have an inexplicable obsession with purple, can't you tell?

Personality

    Playful but chaotic. Loves long walks along the shores of Cape Claw, snacks, mostly carrots, and finding the true reason why earphone cables tangle in your pocket.

Fun Facts

    I watch movies till the after credits. I'm usually one of the last ones out of the theatre. I think the tutorial should always be skippable.(Except in Driver - get gud)
